"Creating a Sustainable Society" is a series of events that takes place monthly in London.
The aim of the initiative is to create a synergy among people and organisations working towards a better society but in different fields.
Each event will have a theme and the speakers will be writers, researchers, people involved in ethical projects or activities, as well as representatives of NGOs.
We wish to create a platform of discussion where the solutions to a sustainable future will be analysed and compared by a panel of experts.
The following are some of the subjects that will be discussed in the series of events:
globalisation (global governance, international trade, international capital flows, WTO, GATS)
global warming
sustainable cities & communities
renewable energies
waste reduction & recycling
food production
agriculture & biodiversity
production & consumption (localisation)
monetary reform (interest-free banking, complementary currencies, seigniorage reform, national & international debt)
taxes and subsidies
traffic & public transport
global commons
ecological debt
from new-liberalism to new spiritual values
